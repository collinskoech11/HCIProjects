A currency converter is basically software code that is designed to convert one currency into another in order to check its corresponding value.
it forms a mobile app and it is based on current market or bank exchange rates. This Currency converters aims to maintain real-time information on current market or
bank exchange rates, so that the calculated result
changes whenever the value of either of the component currencies does.
